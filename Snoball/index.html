What is Snöball?

Snöball is a peer-to-peer marketing platform that helps event organizers mobilize speakers, exhibitors, sponsors, and attendees to promote events through personalized landing pages and social sharing campaigns. Starting at $5,497 per project, their "white-glove" managed service approach handles campaign setup and execution—making it attractive for enterprise teams who prefer a hands-off solution for pre-event promotion.

Where Snöball Fits Well

Snöball works best for teams focused specifically on pre-event peer-to-peer marketing who want a managed service approach.

Managed Service Model

Their white-glove approach means minimal effort required from your team. Campaign setup, monitoring, and optimization are handled by Snöball's team.

Personalized Landing Pages

Creates unique promotional pages for each advocate, giving speakers and sponsors branded assets they can share with their networks.

Referral Tracking

Transparent analytics showing who referred whom, conversion rates by channel, and overall campaign performance metrics.

Email Campaign Integration

Automated follow-up sequences from registration confirmation through pre-event reminders to maximize advocate engagement.

When Each Platform Works Best

The right choice depends on your specific situation and goals.

Consider Snöball When...

  • You only need pre-event peer-to-peer marketing
  • Your team prefers a fully managed, hands-off service
  • You're running a single high-budget enterprise event
  • You don't need during-event photo capture or post-event content engagement
  • You have an existing solution for event-day content and follow-up
Recommended for Full ROI

Consider Premagic When...

  • You want to maximize ROI across the entire event lifecycle
  • You need both advocacy marketing AND event-day engagement
  • Photo capture and instant delivery would enhance attendee experience
  • You want unified analytics connecting pre, during, and post-event metrics
  • You run multiple events annually and need scalable efficiency
  • Extended post-event engagement is valuable for your brand

The Hidden Cost of Point Solutions

Using separate tools for each lifecycle stage creates compounding inefficiencies.

Data Fragmentation

An attendee exists as separate records in your advocacy tool, photo platform, and CRM. Manual reconciliation takes hours and attribution gaps remain.

Workflow Discontinuity

Pre-event tools don't know who actually showed up. Photo systems don't know who's a VIP. Each gap requires manual intervention.

Analytics Silos

No single dashboard shows the complete picture: which advocacy poster drove registrations, who shared photos, and what the total ROI was.

Cost Comparison at Scale

Assembling Snöball ($5,497/project) + a photo platform ($500-1,500/event) + post-event tools often exceeds a unified platform while creating more operational friction and missing the compound effects of connected data.
