What is InGo?

InGo is a social referral marketing platform for events, founded in 2013 and headquartered in Arlington, Virginia. It uses an AI-powered social graph to help event organizers turn registered attendees into advocates who refer their professional connections. Key features include personalized referral cards, "Who's In" networking widgets, network notifications, and gamification leaderboards. InGo's focus is squarely on the pre-event registration phase — leveraging social proof to drive new signups through peer influence.

Key Details

  • Pricing: Starts at $18,000/year (Essential) up to $36,000/year (Professional) — based on number of advocates
  • Primary Channel: LinkedIn social referrals and network notifications
  • Service Model: Self-service software platform — execution, design, and optimization are handled by your team
  • Company Size: ~42 employees with $2.7M in total funding

Where InGo Fits Well

InGo works best for teams focused specifically on driving event registrations through social network referrals.

Social Graph Referrals

Uses an AI-enhanced social graph of connections to suggest relevant people for attendees to invite, going beyond simple share links.

Network Notifications

Automatically alerts people when someone in their professional network registers, creating social proof that encourages signups.

Gamification

Built-in leaderboards and incentive systems to motivate top advocates. Tracks and ranks referral activity across participant groups.

"Who's In" Widget

Embeddable widget on your registration page showing which industry peers are already attending, adding social proof at point of decision.
